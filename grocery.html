Crown Daisy

This is a nutrient-rich leafy green packed with antioxidants, vitamins A and C, and fiber. It supports digestion, boosts immunity, and adds a unique, slightly peppery flavor to soups and stir-fries.

Egg

Are a great source of high-quality protein, essential vitamins, and minerals like B12, choline, and iron. They support brain function, muscle growth, and heart health while being versatile and easy to prepare.

Banana

Bananas are rich in potassium, fiber, and vitamins B6 and C, promoting heart health, digestion, and energy levels. They are a natural source of antioxidants and provide a quick, nutritious snack.

Grape

Grapes are packed with antioxidants, vitamins C and K, and hydrating properties. They support heart health, boost immunity, improve brain function, and promote glowing skin while being a delicious and refreshing snack.
