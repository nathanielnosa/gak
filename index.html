Kitchen

Pounded Yam

is a smooth, stretchy, and delicious Nigerian delicacy made from boiled yams. It is rich in carbohydrates, providing energy, and pairs well with soups like egusi, ogbono, and vegetable soup.

Jollof rice and chicken

is a flavorful West African dish made with rice cooked in a rich tomato sauce, spices, and seasonings. Served with crispy or juicy chicken, it’s a delicious, satisfying meal perfect for any occasion.

Ewaagoyin (Beans)

is a popular african dish consisting of soft, mashed beans served with a spicy, flavorful pepper sauce. Known for its rich taste and unique texture, it's often enjoyed with bread or fried plantain.

Amala

is a african swallow made from yam flour, cassava flour, or plantain flour. It has a smooth, stretchy texture and is typically served with soups like ewedu, gbegiri, or ogbono.

Cat-Fish Pepper Soup

is a spicy and flavorful Nigerian delicacy made with fresh catfish, rich herbs, and aromatic spices. It's a warming, protein-rich dish often enjoyed as a starter or main meal.

Plantain and Egg

is a delicious and nutritious meal featuring fried or boiled plantains paired with scrambled or fried eggs. It’s rich in fiber, vitamins, and protein, making it a satisfying dish.
